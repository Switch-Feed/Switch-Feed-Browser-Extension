Welcome to Switch Feed, a browser extension for facebook.

What is Switch Feed?

This extension adds an anonymous posting board to your Facebook News Feed. You will be able to see posts from anyone who has this extension regardless of if they are in your Facebook friend network. The purpose of Switch Feed is to provide a liberating, uncensored space to talk about things within Facebook.

Toggling Switch Feed

The board can be toggled on and off by clicking the switch feed button at the top of your news feed.

Beta

This is a beta version! Current features are limited to posting, commenting, and browsing the available boards. Each board is currently limited to 25 active posts. New posts will bump old posts out.
